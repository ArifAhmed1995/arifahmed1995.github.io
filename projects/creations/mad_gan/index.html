Study Project: Multi-Agent Diverse GANs

Multi-Agents GANs are a simple modification of the original architecture. Instead of one generator, there are multiple ones and they provide for a realistic variance in output. Each of the generators eventually collapse to different peaks of the distribution to be learned. This was a study project to understand GANs and MAD-GANs. Detailed information can be found in the report. [Report]

Project done under supervision of Prof. JK Sahoo
